About CIA®

With constant changes in the corporate tax regime, legislation, mergers, and compliance, internal auditors are in greater demand than ever.

Certified Fact:

With 50 years of history as the only globally recognized certification for internal auditors, the Certified Internal Auditor (CIA) designation proves that you:

  • Possess current knowledge of The IIA's International Professional Practices Framework (IPPF)
  • Are able to perform an audit engagement in conformance with International Standards
  • Understand organizational governance and can evaluate risks and controls
  • Can apply the business, IT, and management acumen needed for internal auditing

About IIA

The Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA), established in 1941, is the internal audit profession's globally recognized advocate, educator, standard setter, and certifying body.

Why Become Certified?

Becoming a Certified Internal Auditor® (CIA®) is the optimum way to communicate knowledge, skills, and competencies to effectively carry out professional responsibilities for any internal audit, anywhere in the world.
